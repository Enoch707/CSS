What is SMART?

There has been an explosion of OSINT resources on the start.me bookmarking platform, spread across hundreds of community pages. SMART spiders those pages nightly, deduplicates the links, and presents everything in one searchable table. The Mentions column shows how many different start.me pages reference that resource — a rough signal for community popularity. Read more.
